Introduce

Bambu lab A1 mini

Build Volume: 180 × 180 × 180 mm Core Features: Extremely easy setup (pre-assembled, ~20 minutes to first print).
Full-auto calibration (bed leveling, Z-offset, vibration, flow dynamics, etc.) — almost no manual tuning needed.
High-speed printing with up to 500 mm/s toolhead speed and 10,000 mm/s² acceleration.
Active Flow Rate Compensation (nozzle pressure sensor for smoother corners and better quality).
Quiet operation (under 48 dB in silent mode with active noise canceling).
Quick-swap nozzle (0.4 mm standard; supports 0.2 / 0.6 / 0.8 mm).
Built-in camera for monitoring and timelapse.
Supports PLA, PETG, TPU (and some others); not ideal for high-temp enclosure-needed filaments like ABS.
Heated bed up to 80°C, hotend up to 300°C.
Compact footprint — great for desks or small spaces
